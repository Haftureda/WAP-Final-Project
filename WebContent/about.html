About us

Home

We come from many places, cultures, and backgrounds, but share a strong common commitment to personal inner growth, wellness, sustainability, and positive values. We support a progressive and inclusive campus culture that’s creative, dynamic, and focused on making the world a better, more peaceful place. Through Consciousness-Based Education, you’ll study the subjects you’re passionate about while embarking on a journey of self-discovery and inner growth. As your awareness expands, your ability to absorb knowledge and see the big picture improves, and you’ll be able to connect what you’re learning in a meaningful way to your own life.

Computer Science

Do you want to develop your computing skills while growing into the best person you can be? MUM’s computer science programs will prepare you with the skills you need for a successful career while supporting your personal and creative growth with daily meditation, a healthy routine, and a caring community.

Meditation

Our class schedule incorporates daily practice of the Transcendental Meditation technique, which has been proven to reduce stress, enhance clarity of mind, and improve overall health. Many of our students find that daily meditation gives them the clear mind they need to tackle their daily studies and deeply understand complex computer programming dilemmas.

Natural Food and wellness

At MUM, self-care is a priority. You’ll learn highly effective tools — including yoga postures, ayurveda self-pulse and more — to promote a healthy lifestyle.
